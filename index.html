About the club

Structured training. Real community.

Kalos Track Club is a free weekly interval session at the Campbell Community Center Track. Sessions run every Thursday evening and are built around structured threshold and speed work, with groups split by pace so everyone is working at the right intensity. We started in March 2026 and have grown entirely by word of mouth. Show up once and you'll understand why.

What to expect

Here's how a session runs

01

Warm-Up

15 minutes of easy laps and dynamic stretches. 5PM sharp, no waiting around.

02

The Workout

Threshold reps split by pace group: fast, mid, and short. You pick your group. Efforts are 3-8 minutes each with structured rest. We finish with speed work: short, sharp, and optional.

03

Cool Down

Easy jog, full-body stretch, and a few minutes to catch breath together. We're off the track between 6:30 and 6:45PM. Most weeks a group heads somewhere nearby afterwards.
